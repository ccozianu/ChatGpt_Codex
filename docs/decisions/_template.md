# D-NNNN: Short Decision Title

## Context

What forced the choice. State the constraint and the pressure, not the
solution. A reader who disagrees with the decision should still agree with
this section.

## Options Considered

One subsection per real option, each with an honest cost. Options nobody would
seriously pick are padding; leave them out.

### Option A: ...

Cost: ...

### Option B: ...

Cost: ...

## Decision

The chosen option, stated so a future reader can check whether it was actually
followed.

## Rationale

Why this option beat the others. This is the section future readers come for.
Preserve the reasoning that would otherwise die in chat history.

## Consequences

What becomes true, what becomes harder, and what is accepted as lost.

## Reopen If

The observation or evidence that would invalidate this decision.
